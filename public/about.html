About Us


Pawsome Rescues is a Non-Profit dog shelter that was founded in 2015 and is currently located in Arima, Triniad. Our dog shelter strives to nuture and provide uttermost care, love and protection for dogs and puppies of all breed types. Our work and mission sets to achieve the goal of finding safe, loving homes for sheltered dogs and educating the general public on the proper care of adopted and fostered dogs. The Pawsome Rescues Dog Shelters partners with local volunteers and rescue groups to locate and assist abused or stray dogs. Our shelter provides top-notch vetinary services, meals, bedding and toys for our dogs. Additionally, our shelter has successfully re-homed and fostered over 360 dogs.
